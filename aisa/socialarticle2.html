Fun activities to do

Personally I always recommend to walk around the nearby parks if the weather is nice. first of all because it's healthy, second of all because it always refreshes the brain. Even a nice picnic in the park can boost your mood immidiately, and it is also usually entirely free.
Great parks in Eindhoven:

  • Genneper Parken
  • Anne Frankplantsoen
  • Stadswandelpark
  • Phliips De Jongh Park
  • Henri Dunant park
  • Phliips van Lennep Park
  • Gender park

If you would like to learn something new or do something special nothing is better then to check out some museums.
For an eveining program you can go to the cinema, it can be even better because the TU/e has an own cinema for discounts for students.

  • Pathé Eindhoven - On Monday you go to the latest movies with your student pass with a discount.
    This way you can get a ticket from 9 euros as a student!
  • Vue Cinema - Every Thursday is student night at vue cinemas (Alkmaar, Amersfoort, Arnhem, Eindhoven,
    Nijmegen, Deventer and Den Bosch)!!
    As a student, you always go to the movies at these Vue locations* for only €10.50.
    But on Thursday we unpack extra during the STUDENT NIGHT: TWO BEERS FOR THE PRICE OF ONE!!
  • LAB-1 - Also has Student discount: from €6.75 if you show your student pass
  • The Black Box - Movie Theater - evey movie is 4.50 for students.

Go to a concert

  • Muziekgebouw Eindhoven -A Spot ticket costs €10 and can be used last minute. This means that you can buy a ticket up to 24 hours before the start of a concert or festival of Muziekgebouw Eindhoven.
