About Us

The Ordinary Minds - Who are we?

Who are we? We are The Ordinary Minds. We help students with university life. We started this with the help of experienced teachers and energetic students. Our goal is to give you the information, advice, and tools you need to do well in your studies but also to manage your daily life in the Netherlands.
